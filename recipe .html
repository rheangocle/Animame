Ingredients

  • pork
  • rice
  • nori
  • sesame
  • sake
  • miso
  • tsuyu

Recipe

1. Place the rice in a bowl and cover with water. Bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover and simmer for 10 minutes.

2. Meanwhile, in a large bowl, mix the pork, nori, sesame, sake, miso and tsuyu.

3. Add the rice and mix well.

4. Serve with the sauce.
